Our Team

Karina Delmoro, RPN, Owner

Karina Delmoro, RPN has been a Registered Practical Nurse since 2014. Since then, she has worked in numerous clinical settings while owning and operating the Lash & Permanent Make Up business known as Absolute Beauty Spa Salon.

Her passion for the beauty industry now lies on the more Medical side of things. With her in-depth Nursing knowledge, she is pursuing ongoing education and a full time career in Medical Cosmetic Injecting and Medical Cosmetic treatments. She has a lifelong membership with a well known medical company that provides extensive education to Nurses looking to develop their Cosmetic menu with injections and treatments. This lifelong membership allows her to access the newest and most up to date knowledge on all things Cosmetics whenever necessary and ongoing. All the learning comes with hands on experience from the some of industry leading Injectors in Canada.

Karina is in good standings and registered with the College of Nurses of Ontario.

Janet Wilson, Senior Medical Aesthetician

Janet graduated from Gina's College of Advanced Aesthetics as a Medical Aesthetician with honours. She holds plenty of knowledge on skincare and is really looking forward to helping patients achieve their skincare goals.

Janet has been well trained on AlumierMD, Vivier Medical Grade Skincare as well as all of our technology treatments to serve you best.

Committed to the health and safety, Janet has also taken Infection Control certificates to ensure safety as it is our number one priority.
